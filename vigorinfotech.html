About Vigor Industries Limited


Vigor Industries Limited is a globally renowned Indian conglomerate committed to transforming the future through innovation, technology, and sustainability. Founded by visionary industrialist Abhishek Tiwari and co-led by Sanjeevani Tiwari, the company has grown into a powerhouse across multiple sectors.


Our Vision


To lead the world into a new era of industrial innovation by building sustainable solutions across infrastructure, mining, space, defense, healthcare, energy, and technology sectors. We aim to become the world's most impactful conglomerate by 2060, focused on empowering the future of India and the globe.


Mission


  • Develop world-class infrastructure and smart cities, including Vigor City Bhilai and Vigor Sanjeevani Islands.
  • Lead the energy transition through nuclear, solar, hydrogen, and AI-powered power grids.
  • Build India’s strongest indigenous defense and space technologies.
  • Promote ethical AI, sustainability, and health innovations.
  • Invest in education, research, and social transformation at the grassroots level.
