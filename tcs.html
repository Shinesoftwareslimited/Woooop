1. Which company has the tagline "Think Different"?
a) Microsoft
b) Apple
c) IBM
d) Google
Answer: b) Apple

2. Who are the co-founders of Google?
a) Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak
b) Bill Gates and Paul Allen
c) Larry Page and Sergey Brin
