5 teachers recieving best teacher award

Teachers are a backbone of any society. Some teachers take teaching as their job whereas some teachers take it as a passion. They come up with innovative ideas which increases the participation of students in studying. To encourage such teachers Government of India gives awards to such teachers. So come let's see about some of them.

Dr.Priya Sharma

Subject: Mathematics

About:

Dr. Sharma has introduced innovative teaching techniques that have significantly improved student engagement and understanding in mathematics across various schools in India.

Mr.Raj Patel

Subject: Science

About:

Mr. Patel is renowned for his hands-on science experiments and his dedication to fostering a love for science among his students.

Ananya Gupta

Subject: English

About:

Ms. Gupta's creative methods and passion for literature have helped her students excel in English and develop a deep appreciation for reading.

Arjun Mehta

Subject: History

About:

Mr. Mehta's engaging storytelling and thorough historical knowledge have made history lessons exciting and memorable for his students.

Neha Singh

Subject: Art

About:

Ms. Singh has inspired creativity in her students with her artistic techniques and dedication to nurturing young artists.
