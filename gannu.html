Mumbai

Talking about Ganesh Chaturthi and not including cities from Maharashtra would be an unfair practice. Maharashtra and specially mumbai and neighbouring districts are know specially for their Ganesh chaturthi celebration. People from all across the India and globe come their to celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi with Mumbaikars. So if you get an opportunity then must visit Mumbai.

Pune

Pune as being a maharashtrian city, it is quite obvious for it to be at the highest position in this list. People from all over the country come to see the beautiful colors of this festival.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad is also one t of those cities where ganesh chaturthi is celebrated with full enthusiasm. Hyderabadi celebration is known for its unique culture and art style. People especially visit Hyderabad to see kairatabad Ganesh Ji.

Bengaluru

Bengaluru City celebrates Ganesh Chaturthi with public installations of idols, cultural events and huge procession. With a popular spot Ulsoor lake for Ganesh Visarjan.

In Chennai, Ganesh Chaturthi is celebrated across the city with thousands of pandals (temporary stages) housing Ganesh idols. Popular immersion spots include Marina Beach

Bhilai

Talking about the cities which does not belong to Maharashtra. Bhilai a city in chattisgarh is known for its steel plant. This twin city has a diverse population including people from different parts of India, came here to work. Every year this twin city celebrates this beautiful festival with great joy.
